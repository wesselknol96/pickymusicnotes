# Component Names

Use these names when asking for layout changes:

- **Header Block**: the top title area with "Picky Music Notes".
- **Instrument Bar**: the Acoustic Guitar and Piano buttons.
- **Note Block**: the full note/type/variant selector panel.
- **Note Row**: the row with A, A#, B, C, etc.
- **Type Row**: the row with major, minor, sus4, etc.
- **Variant Row**: the row with open chord / bar chord.
- **Chord Card**: the full panel containing the chord title, tab rail, and neck.
- **Chord Title**: the "A (Open Chord)" title.
- **Tab Rail**: the vertical tab circles left of the neck.
- **Acoustic Guitar Neck**: the horizontal fretboard with strings, frets, labels, and finger circles.
- **Request Panel**: the separate request/login panel on the right.
